# AI Cost vs ROI

Evaluating AI cost against ROI involves comparing the total investment in AI systems against the measurable financial benefits generated through cost reduction, efficiency gains, error elimination, and revenue improvement.

## How It Works
Input: AI system development, integration, and management costs alongside current operational cost and revenue metrics
Processing: ROI analysis compares investment against savings and revenue impact over defined periods to calculate net return
Output: ROI calculations, payback period estimates, and total value generation projections for AI system investment

## Use Cases
- Calculating the payback period for an AI customer support system against current staffing costs
- Comparing AI investment against the cost of equivalent outsourcing or hiring
- Projecting three-year ROI from a multi-phase AI deployment program
- Building a business case for AI investment for leadership and board approval
- Validating actual ROI against projections after six months of AI system operation

## Benefits
- Provides financial clarity for AI investment decisions
- Demonstrates the superior long-term economics of AI versus alternative capacity solutions
- Creates accountability for AI system performance against defined financial targets
- Supports business case development for leadership and investment decisions
- Validates ROI delivery and identifies opportunities to improve AI system value

## FAQ
### What is the typical payback period for AI system investment?
Most AI systems achieve payback within three to nine months through labor savings and efficiency improvements.


### How is AI system ROI calculated?
ROI = ((Total benefits generated - Total AI investment) / Total AI investment) x 100, measured over a defined period.


### What costs should be included in an AI ROI analysis?
Development, integration, data preparation, training, deployment, ongoing management, and any operational transition costs.


### What benefits should be included in an AI ROI analysis?
Labor savings, error reduction value, process acceleration revenue impact, retention improvement, and revenue growth enabled by AI.
