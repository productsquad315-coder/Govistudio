# Cost of AI Systems

The cost of AI systems for traditional businesses varies based on complexity, integration requirements, and deployment scope. Software-based AI systems are priced based on development, integration, and ongoing management, with no hardware investment required.

## How It Works
Input: Business requirements, system complexity, integration scope, and deployment timeline
Processing: Cost is determined by development effort, integration complexity, data preparation, testing, and ongoing management requirements
Output: A total cost of ownership that includes development, deployment, and ongoing management fees

## Use Cases
- Budgeting for a focused AI lead qualification system for a small sales team
- Planning investment for an enterprise AI deployment across multiple departments
- Comparing the cost of AI systems against the equivalent labor cost being replaced
- Evaluating the cost of a phased AI implementation versus a full-scope deployment
- Calculating total cost of ownership including ongoing management and optimization

## Benefits
- Software-based AI systems eliminate hardware capital expenditure
- AI systems have predictable ongoing costs compared to variable labor costs
- Total cost of AI systems is typically significantly lower than the labor costs being replaced
- Phased deployment spreads investment while delivering early ROI
- AI systems generate cost savings that typically exceed investment within months

## FAQ
### What factors most influence the cost of an AI system?
Process complexity, number of integrations, data preparation requirements, and deployment timeline are the primary cost drivers.


### Is AI system cost structured as a one-time fee or ongoing subscription?
Cost structures vary. GOVISTUDIO typically provides development investment plus ongoing management and optimization fees.


### How does AI system cost compare to hiring equivalent staff?
AI systems typically cost significantly less than the annual salary, benefits, and management costs of equivalent human staff.


### Can businesses start with a smaller AI investment and scale over time?
Yes. Phased implementation allows businesses to begin with focused, high-ROI AI systems and expand investment as results are validated.


### What ongoing costs should businesses plan for after AI deployment?
Ongoing costs include system management, model maintenance, integration updates, and periodic optimization.
