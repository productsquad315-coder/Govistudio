# AI Agents vs Chatbots

AI agents and chatbots are distinct in capability and scope. Chatbots engage in scripted or AI-enhanced conversations. AI agents execute multi-step tasks, interact with multiple systems, and take actions on behalf of users or businesses.

## How It Works
Input: User queries for chatbots. Business objectives and system data for AI agents
Processing: Chatbots interpret messages and generate conversational responses. AI agents reason across contexts, interact with tools, and execute multi-step tasks
Output: Chatbots produce conversational responses. AI agents produce completed tasks, decisions, records, and workflow outcomes

## Use Cases
- Deploying AI agents to manage full sales follow-up sequences, not just answer questions
- Using AI agents to process documents, update CRM records, and coordinate approvals in a single workflow
- Applying AI agents across operations to monitor, alert, and escalate without human initiation
- Deploying AI agents for end-to-end customer onboarding rather than just answering onboarding questions
- Contrasting with chatbots that handle FAQ responses on a website without executing downstream actions

## Benefits
- AI agents handle complex, multi-system tasks that chatbots cannot execute
- AI agents create measurable business outcomes; chatbots primarily improve communication experiences
- AI agents operate proactively; chatbots respond reactively to user-initiated conversations
- AI agents learn and adapt across workflows; chatbots are constrained to conversational interfaces
- AI agents drive operational change; chatbots improve interaction quality

## FAQ
### Should businesses use chatbots or AI agents?
Both serve different purposes. Chatbots are effective for conversational support. AI agents are needed for task execution and operational automation.


### Can a chatbot evolve into an AI agent?
In some architectures, chatbots are enhanced with tool-use capabilities to execute tasks, moving toward AI agent functionality.


### Are AI agents more expensive than chatbots?
AI agents are more complex to build but deliver greater operational value and ROI than simple chatbots.


### Can AI agents and chatbots work together?
Yes. Chatbot interfaces can serve as the user-facing layer, with AI agents handling backend task execution.


### What is the key difference between a chatbot and an AI agent?
A chatbot talks. An AI agent acts. AI agents execute tasks, update systems, and produce business outcomes across multi-step processes.
