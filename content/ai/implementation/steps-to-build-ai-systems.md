# Steps to Build AI Systems

Building AI systems for traditional businesses follows a defined process from requirements gathering through design, development, testing, integration, and deployment, with ongoing monitoring and optimization after launch.

## How It Works
Input: Business requirements, process documentation, data inventory, and integration specifications
Processing: Development follows structured phases: discovery, design, build, integration, testing, and deployment
Output: A production-ready AI system integrated with existing business tools and delivering defined automation outcomes

## Use Cases
- Building an AI customer support system through a six-week development process
- Developing a custom AI lead qualification system aligned with specific sales criteria
- Constructing an AI document processing system integrated with an existing ERP
- Creating an AI reporting automation system connected to multiple business data sources
- Designing and deploying an AI operations dashboard for multi-department visibility

## Benefits
- Structured development process ensures system quality and reliability
- Defined phases allow for validation and client review at each stage
- Testing and integration validation reduce the risk of post-launch issues
- Phased build approach enables early feedback and adjustments
- Documented development process creates a foundation for future system expansion

## FAQ
### What is the most important phase in building an AI system?
Discovery and requirements definition. A precise understanding of the business problem and desired outcomes is the foundation of a successful AI system.


### How are AI systems tested before deployment?
AI systems go through unit testing, integration testing, user acceptance testing, and performance validation before production deployment.


### What data is required to build an AI system?
Requirements vary by system type. Most AI systems require historical business data, process documentation, and integration access to connected tools.


### How long does it take to build a production-ready AI system?
Simple AI systems are production-ready in two to six weeks. Complex systems may require three to six months.
