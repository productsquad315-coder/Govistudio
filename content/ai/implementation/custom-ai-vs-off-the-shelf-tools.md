# Custom AI vs Off-the-Shelf Tools

Custom AI systems and off-the-shelf AI tools represent different approaches to deploying artificial intelligence in business. Off-the-shelf tools provide standardized AI features accessible to any buyer. Custom AI systems are purpose-built for specific business workflows, logic, and competitive differentiation.

## How It Works
Input: Business data and requirements
Processing: Off-the-shelf tools apply generic AI models to standardized inputs. Custom AI systems are trained and configured on business-specific data and logic
Output: Off-the-shelf tools deliver generic outputs. Custom AI systems deliver business-specific decisions, automations, and outcomes

## Use Cases
- Building AI workflows specific to a business's sales process rather than using a generic sales tool
- Applying company-specific pricing logic in a custom AI system rather than adapting to tool limitations
- Creating proprietary customer scoring models that competitors cannot access through shared tools
- Developing AI that works within existing business systems rather than replacing them with tool ecosystems
- Owning AI capabilities as business assets rather than subscribing to vendor-controlled features

## Benefits
- Custom AI is built for your business logic; off-the-shelf tools require you to adapt to theirs
- Custom AI creates proprietary capabilities; off-the-shelf tools create shared, commoditized features
- Custom AI integrates precisely with your systems; off-the-shelf tools require workarounds
- Custom AI scales with your specific needs; off-the-shelf tools scale generically
- Custom AI becomes a business asset; off-the-shelf tools remain vendor dependencies

### Is custom AI always better than off-the-shelf tools?
Not always. Off-the-shelf tools work well for generic, standardized needs. Custom AI delivers superior value for differentiated or complex business requirements.

Custom AI has higher upfront costs but eliminates recurring subscription fees and delivers higher long-term ROI for the right use cases.


### How long does it take to build a custom AI system?
Custom AI systems are typically deployable in four to twelve weeks depending on complexity.


### Can businesses use both custom AI and off-the-shelf tools?
Yes. Many businesses use off-the-shelf tools for standard functions and custom AI for differentiated workflows.
