# AI Systems vs RPA

AI systems and robotic process automation (RPA) both automate business processes but differ in how they handle variability, intelligence, and scope. RPA follows fixed scripts to automate UI-level tasks. AI systems apply intelligent decision-making, process unstructured data, and adapt to changing inputs.

## How It Works
Input: Structured data for RPA. Structured and unstructured data for AI systems
Processing: RPA executes predefined scripts on user interfaces. AI systems apply machine learning, natural language processing, and adaptive logic
Output: RPA produces deterministic task outputs. AI systems produce intelligent decisions, adaptive workflow outcomes, and continuous improvement

## Use Cases
- Replacing fragile RPA scripts with AI systems that handle document variability
- Applying AI to processes where input formats change, breaking RPA scripts
- Using AI to process unstructured data such as emails that RPA cannot interpret
- Combining RPA for legacy system interaction with AI for intelligent decision-making
- Transitioning from RPA to AI for processes requiring judgment and adaptability

## Benefits
- AI systems handle variable inputs; RPA breaks when formats change
- AI systems process unstructured data; RPA requires structured, predictable inputs
- AI systems improve with use; RPA requires manual recoding to improve
- AI systems are more resilient to system and process changes than RPA scripts
- AI systems deliver broader automation value beyond UI-level task execution

## FAQ
### Should businesses choose AI systems or RPA?
For stable, structured, UI-based tasks, RPA can be effective. For complex, variable, or data-rich processes, AI systems deliver superior results.


### Can AI systems and RPA work together?
Yes. RPA handles legacy UI interactions while AI systems provide intelligence and decision-making in hybrid architectures.


### Is AI more expensive than RPA?
AI systems require greater initial investment but deliver more durable, scalable automation with significantly lower maintenance overhead.


### Why do RPA scripts fail?
RPA scripts fail when application interfaces change, data formats vary, or processes evolve beyond the original script parameters.


### What is the future of RPA in an AI-first world?
RPA is increasingly being replaced or enhanced by AI systems that handle the intelligence, adaptability, and scalability that RPA cannot provide.
