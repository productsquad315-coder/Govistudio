# AI Monitoring

AI monitoring is the continuous observation of deployed AI system performance, including accuracy, processing speed, integration health, and business outcome delivery, to ensure reliable operation and early detection of issues.

## How It Works
Input: Real-time AI system logs, performance metrics, integration health data, and business KPI feeds
Processing: Monitoring systems track defined metrics, detect anomalies, and trigger alerts when performance deviates from thresholds
Output: Performance dashboards, automated alerts, issue reports, and operational health summaries for AI systems

## Use Cases
- Monitoring AI lead scoring accuracy and flagging unexpected scoring patterns
- Tracking AI customer support resolution rates and escalation volumes in real time
- Alerting operations teams when AI document processing error rates exceed thresholds
- Monitoring API integration health between AI systems and connected business tools
- Generating weekly AI system performance reports for management review

## Benefits
- Detects performance issues before they impact business operations
- Ensures AI systems continue to deliver accurate, reliable results over time
- Provides management visibility into AI system health and performance
- Enables proactive maintenance by identifying degradation trends early
- Creates accountability and transparency for AI system ROI delivery

## FAQ
### What metrics should be monitored in an AI system?
Accuracy rates, processing volume, error rates, integration latency, decision quality, and business outcome KPIs.


### How frequently should AI systems be monitored?
AI systems should be monitored continuously with automated alerts and regular human review of performance dashboards.


### Who is responsible for AI system monitoring?
GOVISTUDIO provides monitoring infrastructure and active management. Business teams review outcome KPIs as part of normal management processes.


### What happens when monitoring detects an AI performance issue?
Alerts trigger investigation and root cause analysis, followed by corrective action such as model adjustment, data cleanup, or integration repair.


### Can AI system monitoring be automated?
Yes. Monitoring dashboards, alerts, and reporting are automated components of AI system management infrastructure.
