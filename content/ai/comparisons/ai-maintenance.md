# AI Maintenance

AI maintenance refers to the ongoing activities required to keep software-based AI systems operating accurately, reliably, and effectively after deployment, including model updates, integration management, performance monitoring, and issue resolution.

## How It Works
Input: Operational AI system data, performance metrics, integration health indicators, and business change requirements
Processing: Maintenance activities include model retraining, performance review, integration updates, bug resolution, and configuration adjustments
Output: Continuously accurate, reliable, and business-aligned AI systems delivering consistent performance over time

## Use Cases
- Retraining AI lead scoring models as new conversion data accumulates
- Updating AI integration configurations when connected business tools release updates
- Resolving performance degradation in AI document processing as document formats evolve
- Adjusting AI decision thresholds as business criteria and risk tolerance change
- Monitoring and resolving edge cases that emerge in production AI workflows

## Benefits
- Ensures AI systems maintain accuracy and reliability over time
- Prevents performance degradation caused by data drift and changing business conditions
- Keeps AI integrations functional as connected tools update and evolve
- Addresses emerging edge cases before they impact business operations
- Extends the useful life of AI system investments through proactive management

## FAQ
### How often do AI systems require maintenance?
Most AI systems benefit from monthly performance reviews and periodic model retraining, typically quarterly or semi-annually.


### What is AI model drift and how is it managed?
Model drift occurs when AI performance degrades as real-world data patterns diverge from training data. It is managed through regular retraining and monitoring.


### Who handles AI system maintenance?

### What happens to an AI system if it is not maintained?
Without maintenance, AI systems may experience accuracy degradation, integration failures, and reduced business value over time.


### Can businesses self-manage AI system maintenance?
