# AI Systems for Hospitality

AI systems for hospitality are software-based platforms that automate guest communication, booking management, operations coordination, and performance reporting for hotels, resorts, restaurants, and other hospitality businesses.

## How It Works
Input: Reservation data, guest records, operational schedules, feedback, and revenue metrics
Processing: AI systems automate guest communications, manage booking workflows, and generate operational reports
Output: Automated guest messages, operational alerts, booking confirmations, and hospitality performance dashboards

## Use Cases
- Sending automated pre-arrival, check-in, and post-stay guest communications
- Managing reservation changes and cancellations without front desk involvement
- Routing housekeeping and maintenance requests based on room status and priority
- Generating daily revenue and occupancy reports automatically
- Collecting and organizing guest feedback for management review

## Benefits
- Improves guest experience through timely, personalized communication
- Reduces front desk workload through automated booking management
- Enables faster response to housekeeping and maintenance needs
- Delivers consistent operational reporting without manual data collection
- Scales hospitality operations management without proportional staff growth
