# AI Use Cases in Real Businesses

Every AI conversation eventually comes back to the same question: "Show me where it actually works." This is that answer — real use cases, real business functions, real outcomes.

LEAD QUALIFICATION IN A REAL ESTATE AGENCY

A real estate agency was receiving hundreds of property inquiries each month. Agents spent hours sorting through inquiries, responding to everyone individually, and routing qualified leads to the right team members. Most of that time was wasted on unqualified inquiries.

After implementing an AI lead qualification system, every inquiry is evaluated automatically against defined criteria — property type, budget range, timeline, and location. Qualified leads are routed directly to agents. Low-fit inquiries receive an automated response. Agents now spend their time exclusively on leads worth pursuing.

INVOICE PROCESSING AT A MANUFACTURING COMPANY

A manufacturing company's accounts payable team spent days each month processing supplier invoices manually. Each invoice required data entry, purchase order matching, and manual routing for approval.

After implementing an AI invoice processing system, invoices are received, extracted, matched, and routed automatically. The AP team reviews exceptions and approves the final payment run. Processing time dropped dramatically. Error rates fell to near zero.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT AT A SERVICE BUSINESS

A professional services firm was struggling with high support inquiry volume. Response times were too slow, and staff were spending significant time on questions that had standard answers.

After deploying an AI customer support system, common inquiries are resolved automatically. Complex cases are escalated to human staff with full context. Response times dropped from hours to seconds for the majority of inquiries.

REPORTING AUTOMATION AT A LOGISTICS COMPANY

A logistics company's management team spent hours each week compiling performance data from multiple systems into reports for executive review.

After implementing AI reporting automation, weekly performance reports are generated and distributed automatically every Monday morning. Management receives the same quality of reporting with zero additional staff time invested.

WHAT THESE USE CASES HAVE IN COMMON

Each use case involves a high-volume, repetitive process with defined inputs and measurable outputs. Each connects to existing business tools rather than replacing them. And each delivers measurable ROI within weeks of deployment.
